Culture & Practical Tips

Understanding a few basics will help you feel more comfortable and respectful as you travel.

Greetings & Etiquette

  • Greetings: A warm handshake and eye contact are common. Many people speak English in Kigali.
  • Dress: Casual but modest clothing is appreciated, especially in rural areas and religious sites.
  • Photography: Always ask before taking photos of people or sensitive locations.

Practical Travel Tips

  • Carry a reusable water bottle and stay hydrated, especially in warmer regions.
  • Use mobile money or card payments where possible; carry some local cash for small purchases.
  • Public transport is common, but private drivers or organized tours can save time.
