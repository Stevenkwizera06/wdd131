Website Planning Document

Rwanda Travel Guide

Site Name

Rwanda Travel Guide

This name was chosen because it clearly identifies the website's purpose: to serve as a comprehensive guide for travelers interested in visiting Rwanda. The name is straightforward, memorable, and immediately communicates that the site provides travel information about Rwanda, the "Land of a Thousand Hills."

Optional domain availability: rwanda-travel-guide.org

Site Purpose

The Rwanda Travel Guide website provides comprehensive information and resources for travelers planning to visit Rwanda. The site will offer:

  • Detailed information about Rwanda's geography, culture, and history
  • Travel recommendations including must-visit destinations and attractions
  • Practical travel information such as weather data, currency, and time zones
  • Cultural insights and local customs to help visitors prepare for their trip
  • Interactive features including weather information and travel planning tools

Scenarios

The following questions represent typical inquiries from the target audience (travelers and tourists interested in Rwanda):

  1. What is the best time of year to visit Rwanda for gorilla trekking?
  2. What are the current weather conditions in Kigali, and what should I pack for my trip?
  3. What cultural customs and etiquette should I be aware of when visiting Rwanda?
  4. What are the top tourist attractions and destinations I should include in my Rwanda itinerary?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Forest Green (#1B4332)

Used for: Main headings, navigation bar background, primary buttons, and footer background. This color represents Rwanda's lush landscapes and natural beauty.

Secondary Color: Emerald Green (#40916C)

Used for: Secondary headings, links, hover states, and accent elements. This lighter green provides visual interest and complements the primary color.

Accent Color: Warm Orange (#F4A261)

Used for: Call-to-action buttons, highlights, and important information boxes. This warm color adds energy and draws attention to key elements.

Text Color: Charcoal (#2D3436)

Used for: Body text and paragraph content. This dark color ensures excellent readability against light backgrounds.

Background Color: Light Gray (#F8F9FA)

Used for: Main page background and content sections. This neutral color provides a clean, professional appearance.
